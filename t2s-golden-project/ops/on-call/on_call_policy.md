# On-Call Policy (T2S Starter)

## Severity
- Sev1: outage/customer impact
- Sev2: degraded service
- Sev3: minor/workaround

## Expectations
- Ack Sev1 within 5 minutes
- Status update within 15 minutes
- Mitigation immediately

## Workflow
1. Acknowledge
2. Validate impact
3. Mitigate (rollback/scale/failover)
4. Communicate
5. Document timeline
