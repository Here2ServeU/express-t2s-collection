# SOC2 Controls Checklist (Starter)

Starter checklist for SOC2-aligned operational controls (not legal advice).

## Access Control
- [ ] Least privilege IAM
- [ ] MFA enforced for privileged access
- [ ] Kubernetes RBAC defined

## Change Management
- [ ] CI/CD is auditable
- [ ] GitOps controls deployments
- [ ] Production approvals enforced

## Monitoring & IR
- [ ] Centralized logs/metrics/traces
- [ ] On-call policy exists
- [ ] Incident playbooks exist and tested

## Backup & DR
- [ ] Backups configured
- [ ] DR plan documented and tested
